Home Inspections That Protect You and Your Family

Buying a home is one of the biggest investments you'll ever make. I provide detailed home inspections that give you confidence and peace of mind. With a thorough evaluation of the home's condition, clear reporting, and follow-up support. I help you make an informed decision.

Home Inspection Services

  • Buyer's Home Inspection - Full property evaluation of structure, systems, and safety concerns.
  • Pre-Sale Inspection - Prepare your home for sale and address issues ahead of listing.
  • New Build & Warranty - Walk-throughs to identify construction defects.
  • Mold Testing - Two samples minimum; lab analysis and recommendations.
  • Radon Testing - At least 48-hour continuous monitoring for accurate results.
  • Meth Testing - Surface sampling in high-risk areas and lab-certified results for accuracy
  • Fentanyl Testing - Swab testing of surfaces, bathrooms, kitchens, and HVAC.
  • Sewer Scope Inspection - High-resolution camera to identify cracks, blockages, and corrosion.
  • Water Testing - Drinking water analysis to protect your families health.
